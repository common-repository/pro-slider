== Description ==

*Details : Pro slider plugin is an 3D slider plugin you can make your site more beautiful with pro slider with proslider you can add multiple sliders in your site.
	   When you are uploading the images please take care of that you should upload minmum 7. And when you are uploading images please upload these in the odd number. 
	   Like 7 / 9 / 11 / 13 / 15 etc.
	  

*Admin section : A Easy and Beautiful admin section will help you to track all the slider that you have made by pro slider. You can edit & delete in any of the slider.
		         there is total two pages in the pro slider the first one is Proslider and second one is My Sliders. the proslider page is for uploading images and second is for tracking all the slider.

*ShortCode: You will find the ShortCode in the my sliders page.  

*Support : Pro Slider Plugin is a community-driven project that would not be the same without your feedback.
	   	   If you find any problem or feature request for this plugin, please feel free to contact us at team@dreamreflectionmedia.com ! 

*Premium Version : 	In the Premium version you can add unlimited sliders and unlimited image in any slider and the free support from our team.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to Create Slider? =

First install pro  sider plugin and here you will see two pages in the plugin go to the proslider page first add hte name of the slider and click to open the media files and select multiple images (Press shift for multiple selection) and upload to the database

= Where can I reach the documentation? =

https://dreamreflectionmedia.com/

=How to slider in my page?=

Just simply go to the my sliders page and find your slider and click to copy the ShortCode and paste it to in your pages.

=what should I do if I choose wrong Image?=

In the case you choose wrong image and submit please go to the my sliders page and here you will see a button for edit & delete your slider.

= How do I select multiple images?=

Just simply click on the first image and press shift and click on last image.

=What should I do when I experience any problem?=

Contact us at team@dreamreflectionmedia.com
